About Us

The Wristo Company Limited Journey began with small but confident steps in 1984 as a joint venture between the TATA Group and Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO). Started with an objective to create a Wristo watch for everyone has now grown to be the fifth largest integrated watch manufacturer in the world which was only the beginning of a success story.Wristo Company Limited (Wristo), a joint venture between the Tata Group and the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO), commenced its operations in 1984 under the name Wristo Watches Limited. Wristo is the fifth largest integrated own brand watch manufacturer in the world. Over the last three decades, Wristo has expanded into underpenetrated markets and created lifestyle brands across different product categories. Wristo is widely known for transforming the watch and jewellery industry in India and for shaping India's retail market by pioneering experiential retail.

Chief Executive Officer- Men Watches
